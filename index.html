How a remote EEG partnership works

We integrate with your existing EEG workflow and EMR as simply as possible, so that high-quality subspecialty reports arrive reliably and on time.

1. Introductory discussion

  • Review your EEG volume across neonatal, pediatric, adult, and ICU settings.
  • Clarify coverage needs (weekday, weekend, backup, or subspecialty-only).
  • Align on reporting format, communication preferences, and urgency tiers.

2. Technical setup

  • Secure remote access to your EEG platform (VPN, cloud viewer, or web portal).
  • Confirm how finalized reports flow back into your EMR or reporting system.
  • Define the stat / urgent notification pathway for critical studies.

3. Pilot phase

  • Begin with a defined set of routine, cEEG, or intracranial studies.
  • Collect feedback from neurology, ICU, NICU, and epilepsy teams.
  • Refine structure, terminology, and thresholds based on real-world use.

4. Ongoing coverage

  • Establish stable workflows once your team is comfortable with the process.
  • Review quality metrics and communication periodically.
  • Scale coverage up or down as your program grows or staffing changes.
