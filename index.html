How a remote EEG partnership works

The goal is to plug into your existing EEG workflow with as little friction as possible while maintaining high-quality, clinically useful reports.

1. Initial discussion

  • Review your EEG volume and typical study types.
  • Clarify coverage needs (weekday, weekend, backup).
  • Agree on turnaround times and report format.

2. Technical setup

  • Secure remote access to your EEG system (VPN or cloud viewer).
  • Define how finalized reports return to your EMR or reading workflow.
  • Set expectations for stat or urgent studies.

3. Pilot period

  • Start with a small group of studies.
  • Gather feedback from neurologists and ICU teams.
  • Adjust language or structure to match your preferences.

4. Ongoing coverage

  • Scale coverage based on your clinical needs.
  • Periodic review of quality and communication.
  • Flexibility to adapt as your program grows.
