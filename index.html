Why remote EEG matters

EEG monitoring improves seizure detection, encephalopathy characterization, and supports timely inpatient decision making—especially when events are nonconvulsive or clinically subtle.

Bottom line: Remote EEG interpretation expands access to subspecialty care, supports higher-level hospital capability, and helps prevent transfers driven primarily by EEG availability.
